Knights and Castles Mod

About the Mod

The Knights and Castles Mod enhances your Minecraft experience by introducing a range of medieval-themed entities and items. Features include:

  • Medieval Guards: Protect your villages with these new NPCs.
  • Undead Knights: Battle fearsome foes at night.
  • Noble Knights: Ally with brave warriors for added protection.
  • New Weapons: Equip yourself with lances and katanas.
  • Phoenix: Discover or summon the mythical phoenix.
  • And Much More!

Purpose

This mod aims to enhance the medieval and fantasy aspects of Minecraft, introducing new challenges, allies, weapons, and mythical creatures.

Why Should You Use This?

  • Immersive Experience: Explore a richly detailed medieval world with new characters and items.
  • Enhanced Combat: Enjoy strategic depth with new weapons and enemy types.
  • Fantasy Elements: Encounter the legendary phoenix, adding excitement to your adventures.
  • Village Protection: Safeguard your villages with medieval guards and noble knights.

About The NF Releases

The NeoForge releases are ports, not new modloaders for this mod. While we have expanded compatibility with other mods by porting KC to NeoForge, new versions will still be released with FML. We may eventually switch to NeoForge, but for now, it is just a second modloader version of KC.
